Before You Apply

ACT AI Lab is designed for founders who are serious about building.

Whether you are starting with an idea, a prototype, or an early-stage venture, we look for individuals who are committed to turning concepts into real businesses.

Not every application is accepted, and not every venture becomes a partnership. We review every submission carefully and select opportunities where we believe we can create meaningful value.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a technical background to apply?

No. Many successful founders are industry experts, operators, or entrepreneurs rather than software developers. We work with founders from a variety of backgrounds.

Do I need a finished product before applying?

No. We work with founders at different stages, from early concepts and prototypes to launched products and existing businesses exploring AI opportunities.

Can I apply if I only have an idea?

Yes. Many ventures start with a strong idea and a committed founder. One of our goals is to help entrepreneurs evaluate, refine, and develop promising opportunities.

Do I need a co-founder?

No. Having a co-founder is not a requirement. We review each opportunity based on the founder, the idea, and the potential to execute.

Do I need experience in AI?

No. Industry knowledge and problem understanding are often more important than technical expertise. Our role is to help bridge the gap between ideas and execution.

How much does it cost to work with ACT AI Lab?

Every venture is different. Depending on the scope and stage of the project, engagements may involve advisory services, product development fees, partnership structures, or a combination of these.

Does ACT AI Lab invest in every venture?

No. Investment opportunities are evaluated separately and selectively. Not every project will qualify for partnership or investment consideration.

Can ACT AI Lab become a partner in my venture?

In selected cases, ACT AI Lab may explore long-term partnerships through equity participation, strategic support, or investment. These opportunities are evaluated individually and are never a requirement to apply.

Will ACT AI Lab sign an NDA before reviewing my idea?

We understand the importance of confidentiality. However, most early-stage ideas can be discussed without an NDA. If a project progresses to a deeper evaluation stage, confidentiality agreements can be considered where appropriate.

Who owns the intellectual property?

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, intellectual property ownership and licensing arrangements will be clearly defined as part of any engagement before development begins.

How long does the review process take?

We aim to review applications within 7–14 business days. If we believe there is a potential fit, we will contact you to schedule an introductory discussion.

Can existing businesses apply?

Absolutely. We welcome applications from startups, entrepreneurs, SMEs, and established businesses looking to explore or develop AI-powered products and services.
