01 — The founding idea

We do not believe holding companies should be passive.

For most of the last century, the holding company was a financial construct — a vehicle for capital allocation, with a quarterly cadence and a respectful distance from operations. That model worked when industries moved slowly and capital was the scarce ingredient.

That world is gone. The companies winning today are built by teams who can ship, recruit, regulate, partner and tell a story — all at once. They need capital, but they need operating muscle far more. ACT was built to provide both, in the same hands.

02 — The MENA bridge

A region the rest of the world is finally paying attention to.

The UAE and Saudi Arabia have committed tens of billions of dollars to becoming global hubs for artificial intelligence, advanced technology and consumer wellness. National strategies — UAE 2031 and Saudi Vision 2030 — have moved from policy documents to data centres, sovereign-backed AI companies, and direct partnerships with Microsoft, Nvidia, OpenAI, Google and others. The buyer is awake. The capital is deployed. The consumer is young and digital-native.

Yet most global companies still treat MENA as an after-thought — entered late, served remotely, and governed without local context. That is the gap ACT was built to close.

For international AI, technology and wellness companies, we operate as a regional partner — through advisory engagements, board roles, market-entry mandates, or long-term operating partnerships. The structure varies. The mandate does not: scale here, properly, the first time.

03 — What we focus on

Three categories. One conviction.

Our work concentrates on the intersection of intelligence, performance and health — categories that, viewed individually, are large; viewed together, are foundational.

Artificial intelligence is the substrate. Human performance is the application that touches every person on earth. Health is the outcome that makes both meaningful. The companies we build live at the place these three meet.

04 — How we work

Three ways we create value.

01 / Build

We start companies.

When we see a gap the market hasn't filled, we form the team, write the first cheque, and operate alongside founders from concept to commercialisation.

02 / Invest

We back exceptional teams.

Where founders already have the vision and traction, we provide capital, governance, network, and the operating playbook of a holding company.

03 / Scale

We compound from inside.

We treat scaling as a discipline. Org design, distribution, technology, regulation, and brand — handled as a system, not a series of fires.

05 — Governance

Founder-led. Operator-advised.

ACT is a private holding company, founder-led by design. We move with the conviction and tempo that only direct ownership allows.

We are supported by a circle of senior advisors — operators, scientists, and investors who have built and governed companies at institutional scale. Their counsel informs how we deploy capital, structure ventures, and make the decisions that compound. We will formalise additional governance as the portfolio matures.
