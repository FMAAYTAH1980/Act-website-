LUUCI

Founder & CEO

An AI wellness ecosystem for daily decision-making.

LUUCI pairs personalised AI intelligence with wearable sensing and actionable daily guidance to help individuals make better choices about sleep, nutrition, movement, recovery and metabolic health. The product is built around a single principle: that the most powerful wellness platform is one that learns the individual better than they know themselves.

LUUCI sits at the centre of ACT's thesis — intelligence, performance and health, delivered to the consumer as a single, trusted experience.

One Hundred Group

Advisory Board & MENA Partner

A global platform for trail and endurance running.

One Hundred Group is building the definitive platform for trail and endurance running — host of the Endurance Trail World Championship and connecting athletes with world-class race experiences, destination communities, and the brands that serve them. The platform is designed to scale across geographies while deepening the relationship between sport, place, and audience.

Endurance is the most honest expression of human performance. One Hundred Group is built around that idea — for the athlete, the host city, and the brand behind both.
